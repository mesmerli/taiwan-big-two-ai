Game Rules (大老二規則)

Hands (牌型)

  • Rank: 2 > A > K > Q > J > 10 > 9 > 8 > 7 > 6 > 5 > 4 > 3
  • Suit: Spade ♠ > Heart ♥ > Diamond ♦ > Club ♣
  • Dragon: 13-card sequence. Wins immediately! (2x)
  • Five-card: Str. Flush > 4-of-a-Kind > Full House > Straight

Scoring (計分)

  • Base: 1 point per card left.
  • Winner x2: Ends with "2", Pair, or 5-card hand.
  • Winner x4: Ends with 4-of-a-Kind or Str. Flush.
  • Loser x2: Left with 10+ cards.
  • Loser x2: Per "2" card in hand.
  • Loser x2: Per 4-of-a-Kind or Str. Flush in hand.

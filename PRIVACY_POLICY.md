# Privacy Policy for Taiwan Big Two AI

Last updated: May 14, 2026

## 1. Introduction
"Taiwan Big Two AI" (the "Application") is a card game developed for research and entertainment purposes. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information that might be processed through the Application.

## 2. No Data Collection
We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data, gameplay statistics, or identifying information to our servers. All gameplay data, AI learning notes, and settings are stored locally on your device (using LocalStorage).

## 3. Third-Party AI Integration
The Application allows users to connect to Large Language Model (LLM) APIs (such as LM Studio, OpenAI, or other compatible local/remote servers). 
- **User Responsibility**: When you configure an API URL and Key, the Application will send game-state data (card hands, game history) to that specific endpoint for strategic analysis.
- **Data Handling**: We do not control how those third-party providers handle your data. Please consult the privacy policy of the API provider you choose to use.

## 4. Local Storage
The Application uses your device's local storage to save:
- Game settings (language, character selection).
- AI settings (API URL, Model ID).
- AI learning records (JSON memory).
You can clear this data at any time by clearing your application data or deleting the local storage files.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
